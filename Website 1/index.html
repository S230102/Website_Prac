“We pride ourselves on being the Best Orthopedic and Multispeciality Hospital in Pune, India.”
The Foremost Center for Specialised Surgeries & Critical Care.

The “LIFELINE” is a state of the art, multispecialty hospital. Tertiary care hospital in the upcoming part of Pune city. The aim is to create the centre of life, where holistic health care can be offer under one roof. Hospital is an aesthetically designed modern, airy and spacious structure.


It is run by well-known efficient and professional team of doctors.


The hospital opened its doors for the service of humanity. The hospital is committed to achieve excellence in health care by providing the latest technology for diagnostic and therapeutic services set up with world class ambience.


Employing a team of highly qualified and committed consultants and paramedical staff, combined with state-of-art of technology, the hospital ensures quality medical care to meet the demands of different age groups and risk profiles. The Hospital offers a wide range of tastefully furnished recovery rooms along with panoramic and serene surroundings, homely atmosphere and comfortable accommodation.
